Kinkaku-Ji

This temple of the Golden Pavillion, Kinkaku-ji is a zen Buddhist temple in Kyoto, Japan. After visiting Kinkaku-ji in Kyoto I wanted to create a minimalistic web design explaining about this incredible building to match it’s beautiful zen garden. The area around the temple is very minimalistic surrounded by a beautiful pond with small bonsai trees surrounding the area.


I want to inform the reader with information about this wonderful yet hidden temple in Japan and educate the features of the area, why it is so protected and the history around the temple itself.
