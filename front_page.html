Fidget

Using A3 thick sketch paper, this image was sketched using HB, 2B and 4B pencils to form the shadows of the image. I also used a mono one stick eraser to define the areas of light and any details within the image. This image was a photograph taken by my client.

Escape From The Pie Folk

A recreation of the scene from the cartoon Star Butterfly, using a Wacom Cintiq 13” I sketched this from sketch to completion rendering with Photoshop CC. My style is very cartoony, I wanted to keep the cartoony style as well as a more realistic style.

Tuxedo Mask

A popular image spreading around social media of a screenshot recreation that I participated in. I used a Wacom Cintiq 13” and Photoshop CC and sketched this image from start to finish. The colours really complimented this piece.

Family

A cartoony style family piece my client had commisioned. I sketched this on plain paper to present to the client and used my Wacom Cintiq 13” using Photoshop CC and Adobe Illustrator to render the typography to look crisp and clean across the painting.

Brochure Collections

Over time I have been very lucky to work on some of the most amazing publications as well as creating adverts, catalogues, leaflets and books.

Crufts Dog Show

Official Show Catalogue

Crufts is an international dog show held annually in the United Kingdom. Organised and hosted by the Kennel Club, it is the largest show of its kind in the world. Working alongside a team of designers I was able to work closely with the Kennel Club and its clients to create outstanding adverts. My goal was to create adverts that would stand out to the reader. I used Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Indesign to create these pieces.

Nissan

UEFA Champions League

Nissan is a proud global partner of the UEFA Champions League, allowing fans across the country to enjoy all the fast-paced action as Nissan brings you even closer to the beautiful game. Working alongside Nissan and being able to build a catalogue range with a signature blue which was used for their all electric Nissan Leaf, I had the pleasure to create a number of items ranging from garments to promotional accessories. Each page was tailored to Nissan, this was created in Adobe Indesign, Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop. I also used a 3D rendering program for some of the images within the catalogue.

Gihon Publishing

Advertising for Social Media

As part of Gihon Publishing, I created advertisements on social media for companies and authors. I was in charge of creating all of the graphical content for our clients. This ranged from traditional printed brochures to online adverts and website assets. I wanted to create a nice piece which stood out and using the typography to create a nice smooth piece for the reader. This elevated Gihon Publishing’s numbers and more clients were approaching the company to create content for them.
