Crufts Dog Show

Official Show Catalogue

Crufts is an international dog show held annually in the United Kingdom. Organised and hosted by the Kennel Club, it is the largest show of its kind in the world. Working alongside a team of designers I was able to work closely with the Kennel Club and its clients to create outstanding adverts. My goal was to create adverts that would stand out to the reader. I used Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Indesign to create these pieces.
